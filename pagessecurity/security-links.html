Links

These days while searching the internet it is very easy to come across ads and links that can infect your computer with all sorts of viruses and malware.

  • First step is to never click on ads that warn you of missing updates. Always go to the official sources for your updates and update regularly.
  • Second step is to make sure you have an adblocker in place. Personally, I really like UBlockOrigin which is available on most browsers now.
  • Third is to never click no something that tells you your machine is infected. Because if you do then it will be.
